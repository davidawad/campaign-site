New Jersey is a mess — it's too expensive, our government is inefficient, and our schools aren't good enough.

Let's Move New Jersey Forward

Taxes

The income tax should be 0%

The stock market is a casino. We should reduce the long term capital gains tax to incentivize long term behavior.

We need to incentivize people to take risks. Starting a business should be as cheap as possible until taxes are appropriate.

There must be clear tax breaks for startup companies that help the environment and bring jobs to New Jersey.

Crime

We need to incentivize individuals with exceptional integrity to become officers.

Qualified immunity must be repealed. Government officials must be accountable for violating constitutional mandate.

Too much money is wasted every year prosecuting nonviolent drug offenses and ruining lives. NJ must decriminalize drug use and vacate sentences.

Healthcare

No one should go without healthcare. If we're going to spend money, let's do it compassionately.

We must strategically deregulate healthcare over the next ten years to realign free-market incentives and make healthcare cheaper and more affordable to everyone.

We can't continue without clarity of healthcare pricing. All healthcare providers must make FHIR compliant data feeds available of exact prices for all billing codes and their insurance networks.

Education

We'll give students a free voucher to pick any school they want to attend.

Education is a human right and our children should be able to choose where they receive it.

We have over 1.6 TRILLION dollars of student debt. The ISA model realigns the incentives of universities & students.

NJ will guarantee ISAs so you don’t have to get student loans ever again.

Civil Infrastructure

You should not have to pay someone to help give the government your money.

You should be able to get a tax bill online and pay it in five minutes, even with bitcoin if you want.

NJ can lead the nation by adopting the one trillion dollar blockchain industry for government services.

We need harsh penalties for government accountability. If a public servant is convicted for a crime within their scope of agency they can never serve the public in any capacity ever again.

Electoral Reform

We need appropriate term limits on legislative offices.

We must repeal “sore loser laws,” including rules that make it impossible to run as an independent, after losing a party primary.

NJ must use nonpartisan commissions and artificial intelligence to generate unbaised and fair districts.

We must remove obstacles to legal voter registration and voting by advocating for automatic voter registration, same day registration, online registration, early voting, vote-at-home options, and/or by making election day a holiday.
